What Is Germination?

Germination is a process in which a plant grows out from a seed by obtaining all the suitable and required conditions.

The Flow Of Germination

Every Plant starts out as a seed, This seed will only germinate when it will get the suitable conditions for it to live in, Such as : suitable temperature, suitable pH of soil , Mineral salts and many other things. After germination, the plant will grow into a seedling, and then a flowering plant allowing the plant to be able to reproduce and then a fully grown tree(not all plants become trees)

What Is A Lifecycle ?

Image by : Tolga Ulkan/unsplash

A lifecycle is a way in which an organism changes throughout its lifetime, reproduction is a vital part of the lifecycle as it does not let the particular species go extinct.

Lifecycle Of Plants

Plants start out as a seed, this seed germinates and then becomes a seedling, In order for this seedling to reproduce, The seedling needs flowers, so the next stage would be that of a flowering plant/Mature Plant.
